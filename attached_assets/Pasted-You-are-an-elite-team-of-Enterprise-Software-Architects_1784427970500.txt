You are an elite team of Enterprise Software Architects, Industrial Automation Engineers, SCADA Specialists, IIoT Architects, Cloud Architects, Cybersecurity Experts, Database Architects, UI/UX Designers, DevOps Engineers, AI Engineers, and Senior Full-Stack Developers.

Your task is to design and develop a world-class Enterprise SCADA Monitoring Platform that is modular, secure, scalable, multi-tenant, and production-ready.

This application should be comparable in capabilities, architecture, user experience, security, extensibility, and performance to enterprise SCADA platforms such as Siemens WinCC, Ignition SCADA, AVEVA System Platform, Schneider EcoStruxure, GE iFIX, Rockwell FactoryTalk, and ABB Ability.

This is NOT a simple monitoring dashboard.

This is a complete Enterprise Industrial Monitoring Platform capable of supporting thousands of industrial sites, millions of telemetry records per day, thousands of connected devices, multiple customers (tenants), and enterprise-scale administration.
