# Enterprise Solar SCADA Monitoring & Energy Management Platform – Comprehensive Development Requirement

## Project Vision

Design and develop a **world-class, enterprise-grade Solar SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) & Energy Management Platform** capable of monitoring, controlling, analyzing, and optimizing utility-scale, commercial, industrial, and rooftop solar power plants.

This should **not** be a simple monitoring dashboard. It should be a complete **Solar Operations Management Platform** that combines SCADA, IoT, Asset Management, AI Analytics, Predictive Maintenance, Energy Management, Alarm Management, GIS, Reporting, Mobile Applications, and Enterprise Administration into one integrated SaaS platform.

The application should be suitable for EPC companies, Solar Developers, IPPs (Independent Power Producers), O&M Companies, Utility Operators, Renewable Energy Companies, Government Agencies, and Enterprise Customers.

The platform should provide real-time monitoring, intelligent automation, predictive analytics, fault diagnosis, maintenance management, and operational optimization while ensuring maximum plant efficiency, energy yield, equipment reliability, and regulatory compliance.

---

# Primary Objectives

Develop a production-ready application that enables operators to:

* Monitor entire solar plants in real time.
* Control equipment remotely where permitted.
* Maximize energy generation and plant efficiency.
* Detect equipment failures before they occur.
* Reduce downtime through predictive maintenance.
* Analyze historical performance.
* Monitor weather conditions.
* Track power evacuation.
* Ensure grid compliance.
* Manage maintenance activities.
* Receive intelligent alerts and notifications.
* Generate operational and financial reports.
* Manage multiple plants from a centralized control center.
* Access the system from desktop, tablet, mobile, and control room displays.
