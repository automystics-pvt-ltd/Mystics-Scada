Build a production-ready, enterprise-grade, multi-tenant Solar Monitoring & SCADA SaaS Platform that allows a Super Admin to onboard and manage unlimited customers, solar plants, and IoT devices from a centralized cloud platform.

The platform should support solar plants of any capacity (rooftop, commercial, industrial, utility-scale, 100 kW to 100 MW+) and provide real-time monitoring, analytics, reporting, alarm management, maintenance management, and customer access.

Key Requirements:

- Multi-tenant SaaS architecture with complete customer data isolation.
- User roles: Super Admin, Customer Admin, Plant Manager, Operations Team, Maintenance Engineer, Read-Only User.
- Support onboarding unlimited solar plants and projects.
- Each plant can contain multiple inverters, meters, weather stations, transformers, breakers, sensors, and gateways.

IoT & Device Integration:
- Support RS485 communication.
- Support Modbus RTU, Modbus TCP, MQTT, HTTP APIs, WebSockets.
- Support third-party gateways and devices such as Teltonika, Moxa, Advantech, Siemens, Schneider Electric, Delta, Huawei, Sungrow, Solis, Growatt, Fronius, ABB, and any custom device.
- Create a flexible Device Template Engine that allows onboarding new device models without code changes.
- Device auto-discovery, mapping, diagnostics, online/offline monitoring, health monitoring, firmware tracking, and communication status.

Real-Time Monitoring:
- Live telemetry updates every few seconds.
- Real-time dashboards with WebSocket-based streaming.
- Monitor:

- Weather monitoring:

- Real-time equipment visualization.
