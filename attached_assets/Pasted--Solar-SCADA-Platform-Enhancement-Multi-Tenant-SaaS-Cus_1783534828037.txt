# Solar SCADA Platform Enhancement – Multi-Tenant SaaS, Customer Portal, IoT Device Management & Enterprise User Experience

## Objective

Design the Solar SCADA Platform as a **secure, enterprise-grade Multi-Tenant SaaS application** where multiple organizations (customers) can independently manage and monitor their solar plants from a centralized platform. Each customer must have complete data isolation while the Platform Super Admin manages the overall system.

The application should provide a highly intuitive, modern, and user-friendly interface that enables operators, plant managers, maintenance teams, and customers to monitor, analyze, and manage their solar assets with minimal training.

---

# Multi-Tenant SaaS Architecture

Implement a true **Multi-Tenant SaaS architecture** with complete tenant isolation.

Each customer should have:

* Dedicated Organization (Tenant)
* Multiple Solar Plants
* Independent Users
* Independent Roles & Permissions
* Independent IoT Devices
* Independent Asset Management
* Independent Dashboards
* Independent Reports
* Independent Alarms
* Independent Notifications
* Independent Documents
* Independent Configurations
* Independent Historical Data

No customer should ever be able to access or view another customer's data.

Only the **Platform Super Admin** should have visibility across all organizations.

---

# Customer Portal

Each customer should receive a dedicated login portal where they can access only their organization's information.

Customers should be able to:

* Monitor their solar plants
* View live SCADA dashboards
* Monitor energy generation
* View alarms and faults
* Analyze equipment performance
* Review maintenance activities
* Download reports
* Configure notifications
* Manage their users
* Manage IoT devices assigned to their organization
* View historical trends
* Access AI-generated insights

All information displayed must be filtered automatically based on the authenticated tenant.

# Critical Requirements

* Ensure **100% tenant data isolation** across all modules.
* Preserve high performance while supporting thousands of devices and multiple organizations.
* Allow customers to independently manage their users, devices, reports, and notifications.
* Maintain complete auditability for all user actions and device configurations.
* Design the platform for future scalability, supporting additional renewable energy assets, protocols, and AI capabilities without architectural changes.

## Expected Outcome

Deliver a **world-class, multi-tenant Solar SCADA & Energy Management Platform** where each customer operates within a secure, isolated workspace. Customers should be able to monitor their own plants, configure IoT devices, manage users, analyze performance, respond to alarms, and generate reports through a modern, intuitive interface. The platform should combine enterprise-grade security, powerful SCADA capabilities, advanced analytics, AI-assisted operations, and exceptional user experience, making it suitable for commercial deployment across utility-scale and distributed solar installations.
